Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a popular Italian espresso-based drink characterized by equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk, creating a rich and creamy beverage with a distinctive layer of foam on top. Its balance of strong coffee flavor and velvety texture makes it a beloved choice among coffee enthusiasts worldwide.

Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate is a comforting beverage made by mixing melted chocolate or cocoa powder with hot milk or water, often sweetened and topped with whipped cream or marshmallows. Its indulgent and velvety richness makes it a favorite for cozy moments and chilly weather.

Vodka Tonic

Vodka tonic is a classic cocktail made with vodka and tonic water, typically garnished with a slice of lime. This crisp and refreshing drink is known for its simplicity, making it a popular choice for those seeking a straightforward and light libation.
